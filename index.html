About

Hi, I'm Sinan. I'm a full stack developer mostly using the MERN stack.

I am a web developer and recent graduate of the Full Stack Web Development Career Path bootcamp at Thinkful. I enjoy coding because it allows me to create something from nothing, and I love the feeling of working hard to find solutions to problems involving client and server communication, learning a new framework or library, and anything that increases my knowledge of code.

My detail oriented personality helps me to see the big picture, and the steps necessary to get there while working on projects. When I'm not coding, I am either playing video games, golfing, on the beach, cooking, eating, watching soccer, or lounging with friends. I am a very positive person who enjoys meeting new people and learning from them. I believe if I am a better version of myself today than I was yesterday, then I am going in the right direction.
